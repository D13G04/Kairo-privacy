Privacy Policy – Kairo

Last updated: December 22, 2025

Summary

Kairo does not collect, store, or track personal data.

Data Collection

Kairo does not collect, store, or share personal data. The app does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Support Emails

When you contact support, Kairo may prefill your email message with basic technical information to help troubleshoot issues. This can include:

  • App name and version
  • Operating system version
  • Device type (e.g., iPhone or Mac)
  • Language and time zone

This information is included only when you take an explicit action (for example, tapping “Contact Support”) and is sent through your email provider as part of the message you review and choose to send. Kairo does not automatically send emails, and does not store this information.

In-App Purchases

Kairo uses Apple’s StoreKit framework to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. All payment information is handled by Apple and is never accessible to the developer.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: contact@kairoapp.ch
